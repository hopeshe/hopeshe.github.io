30岁从头开始学英语，打卡

2020-02-27 英语学习 新概念英语 评论

而立之年，从头开始重新学习英语，就此立 flag！

学习英语的目的是为了能够使用这么语言，该学的技能：理解、口语、阅读、写作，讲课顺序是：听到的再说，说过的再读，读过的再写。我选择《新概念英语》作为主要教材，学习完记录打卡。

Lesson 1 Excuse me! 对不起 ！

课文

Excuse me!

Yes?

Is this your handbag?

Pardon?

Is this your handbag?

Yes,it is.

Thank you very much.

笔记

【1.Is this your handbag?】

一般疑问句，升调，这是你的(…)吗？

han(d)bag 两个爆破音连在一起，前一个失去爆破。

【2.Yes,it is.】

肯定回答，也可以回答：This is my (…) 这是我的…

it is 辅音结尾元音开头，连读。

【3.Thank you very much.】

其他同义回答：

  • Thank you.
  • Thanks.
  • Thanks a lot.

【4.Pardon?】

Pardon 是缩写，全句为 I beg your pardon. 意思是：请求对方把刚才的话重复一遍。

Lesson 2 Is this your …? 这是你的…吗 ？

课文

Is this your pen? 钢笔

Is this your pencil? 铅笔

Is this your book? 书

Is this your watch? 手表

Is this your coat? 上衣

Is this your dress? 连衣裙，套裙

Is this your skirt? 短裙

Is this your shirt? 衬衣，男士衬衫

Is this your car? 汽车

Is this your house? 房子

练习

Excuse me.

Yes?

What’s your name?

Pardon?

What’s your name?

My name is John.

Look at the situations.Which expression do you use for each? 针对所给的场景选择你应该说的话。

1.You don’t hear something very well.What do you say?[Pardon?]

2.Someone gives you something.How do you thank him or her?[Thank you very much.]

3.You want to speak to someone.What do you say first?[Excuse me.]

4.You want to greet someone.It is 9.30 a.m.[Good morning.]
